FAQ

What inspires you? Why?

We are inspired by Lord Shiva, who is the biggest donor who does not refuse to give anything to any petitioner. Because donation is the identity of India and charity should be such that one hand is donated and the other hand does not get known.

Why did you start this organization? How has it grown or changed over time?

We start a NGO because we want to have a better world around us. Contrary to normal belief that we start NGO to help others, I think we start NGO to help ourselves. Imagine a world where you are seeing people around you are sufferings without proper food or care and this makes you uncomfortable and then you decide to do something and start a NGO, raise funds, arrange resources etc. So all this you did because as a person you wanted to be happy. Others got benefitted by your act and now may have a chance for a better life. Many times people just not realise this aspect.

Who exactly/what services will my money go to?

The funds contributed by donors and sponsors is used for the good causes by our NGO’s on various programs. We have been associated with this NGO since last few months and We can strongly recommend this NGO to everyone as its one the best leading NGO which is tirelessly working for the betterment and welfare of people. This NGO has started in July 2021 and since then it as achieved many awards and recognition's for their work and helped thousands of people. here are the ways through which our money get involved :

  • Sponsor an orphan child
  • Sponsor midday meals for poor elderly women
  • Monthly groceries for destitute women
  • Sponsor a child’s education
  • Day care centers for poor children
  • Women’s livelihood training
  • Covid-19 relief
  • Volunteer in any useful way
